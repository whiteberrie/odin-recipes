Sirloin Steak

The best tasting sirloin steak

The only way to make a nice sirloin steak.

Ingredients

  • 600 gram sirloin stake.
  • Olive oil and butter.
  • Salt and pepper.
  • Garlic cloves.
  • Thyme and parsley.

How to cook sirloin steak.

  1. Dry and season the steak with salt.
  2. Sear the steak for two minute per side.
  3. Add garlic, herbs and butter and spoon over the steak with melter butter until done.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.

    5. Serve with a fresh salad and potatoes.
