Lasagna

The only lasagna recipe you'll ever need

This is the most popular lasagna recipe on the internet. Try our recipe today to understand why.

Ingredients

  • One kilogram ground beef.
  • An onion and a clove of garlic.
  • A can of crushed tomato, two cans of tomato sauce, and two tablespoons of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of sugar or two tablespoons of honey.
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, pepper.
  • Lasagna sheets.
  • Two tablespoons of butter.
  • Two and a half tablespoons of flour.
  • Five deciliters of milk.
  • Mozzarella.

Bechamel Sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan.
  2. Add the flour and whisk.
  3. Add the milk, a little at a time. Let it boil up while whisking for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Meat Sauce

  1. Set the oven at 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Chop the onion and garlic.
  3. Fry the onion and garlic lightly.
  4. Add the ground beef.
  5. When the meat has some surface, add the tomato sauce, paste and honey and all the spices.
  6. Turn down the heat and let the meat sauce boil slowly, the longer you'll leave it the better it will taste.

Layering

  1. Bechamel sauce.
  2. Lasagna sheet.
  3. Meat sauce.
  4. Bechamel sauce.
  5. Lasagna sheet.
  6. Repeat the layers.

Put the assembled lasagna in the oven for 40 minutes, when 10 minutes remain add mozzarella on top.
