Swedish Pancakes

The best pancakes you'll ever taste.

These pancackes will be the only pancakes you'll ever want to make.

Ingredients (for 8 pancakes)

  • Three deciliters of flour.
  • Six deciliters of milk.
  • Three eggs.
  • Half a tablespoon of salt.
  • Two tablespoons butter.

How to make Swedish pancakes

  1. Whisk half of the milk into the flour. Whisk the remaining milk, eggs and salt.
  2. Let the batter swell for 10 minutes.
  3. Melt the butter and add it to the batter.
  4. Cook thin pancakes.
